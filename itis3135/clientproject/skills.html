Packing Basics

  • Map, compass, or GPS
  • Snacks & electrolytes
  • Light layers & rain protection
  • Emergency blanket or whistle
  • Small first aid kit

Trail Etiquette

  • Yield to uphill hikers
  • Keep dogs leashed
  • Pack out all trash ("Leave No Trace")
  • Stay to the right, pass on the left
  • Respect wildlife & other hikers

Navigation Basics

  • Learn how to read a simple trail map
  • Follow trail blazes and posted signs
  • Download offline maps before hiking
  • Carry a compass or offline GPS app
  • Study the trail route before you leave the parking lot
